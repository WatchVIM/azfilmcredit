How AZ Film Credit works

Designed to slot in between your production accounting and your CPA’s audit, without replacing either.

STEP 1

Create a project & import data

Add your production details, then upload spreadsheets, payroll exports, and receipt PDFs from your bank, card, or accounting software.

STEP 2

Auto-organize & fix what’s flagged

The tool groups expenses into Arizona-eligible categories and flags issues: missing receipts, non-AZ vendors, or unclear descriptions.

STEP 3

Generate a CPA-ready audit package

One click creates a CPA bundle: master ledger, qualified vs non-qualified spend, AZ-only vendor list, and organized receipts—so your CPA can review and sign.

Track your AZ Film Credit package

Use the Status Tracker Portal to follow your production package from intake to final CPA sign-off and submission to the Arizona Commerce Authority.

  • Missing Info: CPA has requested additional documents.

Frequently asked questions

Does this replace a CPA?

No. AZ Film Credit is a prep and organization tool. Your licensed CPA still reviews, adjusts, and signs the final audit documentation required by the Arizona Film Office.

Can I use this for non-Arizona projects?

The structure is designed for the Arizona Motion Picture Production Program, but the core workflow—organizing expenses, matching receipts, and preparing an audit package—can inform other jurisdictions as well.

Is this an official Arizona Film Office product?

Not yet. AZ Film Credit is designed as a tool filmmakers and CPAs can propose, pilot, or adopt in collaboration with the Film Office to standardize submissions.
