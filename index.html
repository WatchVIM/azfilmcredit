For CPAs & Auditors
  • • Pre-cleaned ledger with AZ categories already applied.

  • • Clear separation of qualified vs non-qualified expenses.
  • • Comment loop back to production for missing items.
  • • Optional $399 Certified AZ CPA sign-off for qualifying projects.

Built around the Arizona Motion Picture Production Program

AZ Film Credit is structured around the documentation a CPA expects: detailed ledgers, residency evidence, vendor locations, and proof of in-state spend.

  • • Tracks in-state vs out-of-state vendors and crew.
  • • Groups expenses by category and eligibility status.
  • • Keeps supporting documentation tied to each line item.
  • • Generates a packet-ready folder you can share with your CPA.

What your CPA receives

  • • Master expense ledger (CSV / Excel / PDF)
  • • AZ-only spend summary with category breakdown
  • • Qualified vs non-qualified expenses report
  • • Vendor registry + AZ verification notes
  • • Payroll summaries + support docs
  • • Receipt / invoice archive matched to expenses

Note: AZ Film Credit is a prep tool and does not replace a licensed CPA or legal advice.

Frequently asked questions

Does this replace a CPA?

No. AZ Film Credit is a prep and organization tool. Your licensed CPA still reviews and signs the final documentation.

Is this an official Arizona Film Office product?

No. AZ Film Credit is an independent prep tool. Always confirm current program rules and forms with your CPA.
